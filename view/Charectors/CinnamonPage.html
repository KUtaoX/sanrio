CINNAMOROLL

Cinnamoroll is a white puppy with long ears that enable him to fly. He is shy but friendly, and he flew down from the sky to Café Cinnamon because he was drawn in by the wonderful smell of cinnamon rolls. He now spends most of his time sleeping on the café terrace or curled up in a customer's lap.
